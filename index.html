How a Burger King humiliation turned into a six-figure weekly income with a simple online business that few know about.

Even with a background in the Air Force Academy, Igor Kheifets shares the story of his challenging work journey as a waiter at the well-known fast-food chain Burger King while living in Israel with his parents.

According to him, he faced humiliation from both customers and coworkers on multiple occasions. The job was not only physically demanding but also poorly paid, forcing him to take on two jobs to make ends meet.

Fortunately, looking at where he is today, overcoming these hardships may have been the best thing that happened to this Canadian immigrant.

Unsatisfied with his situation, Igor sought other ways to make more money and finally achieve the life he desired.

That's when he stumbled upon something called E-farming, an online strategy that's not widely known and is used to generate recurring income on the internet.

According to him, E-farming was responsible for completely changing his financial reality, enabling a drastic life change in just four weeks.

When asked about what E-farming is, Igor explains that it has nothing to do with a traditional farm, taking care of animals, or agriculture in general. That's because he does it all from home with his smartphone.

He also mentions that he didn't need to be on social media, be famous, or have thousands of followers. It's not related to cryptocurrencies or any type of investment. E-farming, according to Igor, is a simple and 100% online strategy that anyone can start today.
